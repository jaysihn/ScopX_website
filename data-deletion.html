How to Delete Your Data in ScopX

ScopX gives you full control over your data. You can delete your account or clear your stored data at any time from within the app.

To delete all local app data:

  1. Open the app and go to the User Account screen.
  2. Tap Clear All App Data to remove saved targets, settings, and preferences.
  3. You can also choose Delete Downloaded Maps to remove cached terrain tiles.
